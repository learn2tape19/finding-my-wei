## Business & Strategy Frameworks

### Permission-First Positioning
Lead with value, permission, and clarity rather than hype.

- Avoid aggressive marketing
- Let the work speak for itself
- Build on reputation and referrals
- Clinical voice, not promotional voice

**Applied in**: Tao book positioning, Learn2Tape messaging

### Media Asset Advantage
Using real clinical work as competitive differentiator.

- 1,000s of images from actual clinical settings
- 100s of videos of student demonstrations
- Authenticity that stock photography cannot match
- Strategic content marketing advantage

**Projects**: Learn2Tape, Sidekick Air demonstrations

### Evergreen Over Viral
Content strategy focused on lasting value, not trending.

- Write for readers 5 years from now
- Solve real problems rather than chase novelty
- Build authority through depth
- Compound returns on content investment

## Learning & Teaching Frameworks

### Principles Over Techniques
Teaching the thinking, not just the doing.

- Frameworks that outlast specific techniques
- Understanding over memorization
- Student ability to adapt and innovate
- Long-term transformation vs. short-term skills

**Applied in**: Tao CE course, Learn2Tape curriculum design

### Teaching Methodology
Evidence-informed pedagogy for adult learners.

- Micro-learning (chapters, not monographs)
- Real-world application immediately
- Reflection and integration phases
- Community of practice

## Intellectual OS Principles

### Permanence Over Platform
Store assets where they last, not where they trend.

- GitHub for source of truth
- Markdown for format longevity
- Version control for history
- Resist platform dependency

### Reflection Compounds
Regular review produces exponential improvement.

- Weekly reviews
- Quarterly assessments
- Annual strategic reflection
- Projects improve future projects

### Creation Is Emergence
Every insight can become multiple things.

- Book → Course → Social posts → Research
- Speaking → Papers → Articles
- Product idea → Business model → Teaching
