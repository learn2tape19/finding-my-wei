# Project Atlas Layer Charter

## Authority
Established by Drew Freedman as the research engine of Finding My Wei.

## Purpose
This layer is the **cross-domain research and investigation engine**.

Project Atlas investigates questions, synthesizes findings, and produces reusable knowledge that serves all domains and feeds the Intellectual Estate.

## Scope

### Inclusion Criteria
- Active research investigations
- Research papers and studies
- Clinical frameworks under development
- Methodology experiments
- Investigation notes and working materials
- Synthesized research findings
- Published research outputs

### Exclusion Criteria
- Domain-specific projects → go to 05_DOMAINS/[domain]/
- Permanent knowledge → goes to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE
- Operational procedures → go to 01_OPERATING_SYSTEM
- Marketing campaigns → go to 04_CAPABILITIES/MARKETING
- Completed, published work → moves to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE

## Governance

**Authority:** Drew Freedman (strategic direction), Claude (project management)

**Investigation Lifecycle:**
1. Investigation is proposed (with research question)
2. Scope and methodology are defined
3. Investigation proceeds (working materials stay in PROJECT_ATLAS)
4. Findings are synthesized (typically 6-24 months)
5. Output is published or moved to INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE

**Decision Speed:** Deliberate (research-grade rigor)

## Lifecycle States
- **CREATION** → Research question posed, scope defined
- **REFINEMENT** → Active investigation, working materials
- **STABLE** → Investigation complete, findings synthesized
- **PUBLICATION** → Research is published or shared
- **PRESERVATION** → Significant research moves to INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE

## Relationships
- **Sources from:** 05_DOMAINS (domain-specific questions)
- **Sends to:** 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE (permanent findings)
- **Uses services:** 04_CAPABILITIES/RESEARCH, ANALYTICS, SYNTHESIS, QUALITY_ASSURANCE
- **Serves:** All domains with synthesized knowledge
- **Reports to:** 00_CONSTITUTION (must comply with governance)

## Success Metrics
- Every investigation has a clear research question
- Investigations produce publishable findings
- Findings are synthesized into frameworks
- Research directly serves domain needs
- Methodology is rigorous and documented
- Results move to permanent estate
