# Sidekick Air Technical Specifications

Author: Drew Freedman
Date: March 2026

## Tabletop Dimensions

Length: 72 inches
Width: 30 inches
Thickness: 4 inches

## Construction

Drop-stitch inflatable structure using coated textile layers connected by internal threads.

## Material System

Preferred: TPU-coated woven drop-stitch

Alternative: PVC-coated drop-stitch

Advantages of TPU:

- lower weight
- greater durability
- premium positioning

## Operating Pressure

10–15 PSI

## Load Capacity

Target capacity: up to 650 lbs

## Valve System

Includes:

- primary inflation valve
- pressure relief valve

## Seam Construction

RF welded or heat welded seams designed to withstand repeated pressurization cycles.

## Attachment System

3M Dual Lock mushroom fasteners used for modular attachments.

## Accessory Interfaces

- face cradle module
- width expanders
- length extenders
- resistance band anchors

## Stand System

Collapsible multi-leg support frame inspired by centipede-style work stands.

Design goals:

- high stability
- lightweight structure
- fast setup
- compact folded form

## Portability Targets

Total weight goal: under 26 lbs

Packed dimensions estimate:

30 x 6 x 6 inches

## Carry System

Rolling travel case with pump and accessory storage.

## Inflation System

Manual or electric pump

Target inflation time: under 3 minutes

## Durability

Must withstand repeated inflation cycles, therapist loading, and environmental exposure.

## Cleaning

Surface compatible with disinfectant wipes and medical cleaning solutions.
