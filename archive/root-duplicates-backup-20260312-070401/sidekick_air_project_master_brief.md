# Sidekick Air / StitchCore Project Master Brief

Author: Drew Freedman
Date: March 2026

## Overview

Sidekick Air is a portable inflatable therapy table platform designed to solve portability and stability problems found in traditional massage tables.

The project evolved into a broader platform called StitchCore, aiming to create a category of rigid inflatable human-support systems.

## Core Problem

Traditional therapy tables:

- weigh 28–35 lbs
- difficult to transport
- require complex setup
- sacrifice stability when lightweight

Sidekick Air aims to provide:

- professional treatment stability
- reduced transport burden
- faster setup and breakdown
- modular expandability
- travel-ready portability

## Core Technology

High-pressure drop-stitch inflatable construction using coated textile materials.

When pressurized, thousands of internal threads maintain structure rigidity.

Applications include:

- therapy tables
- emergency stretchers
- medical gurneys
- sports recovery platforms
- portable rehabilitation systems

## Product Architecture

Components include:

- inflatable drop-stitch tabletop
- collapsible multi-leg support stand
- modular accessory system
- travel transport system
