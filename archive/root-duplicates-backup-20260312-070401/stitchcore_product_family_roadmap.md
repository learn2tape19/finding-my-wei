# StitchCore Product Family Roadmap
Author: Drew Freedman
Date: March 2026

## Purpose

This document outlines the long-term product ecosystem built on AeroStitch Core Technology developed by StitchCore.

While Sidekick Air represents the first commercial product, the underlying technology platform enables a broader family of inflatable structural support systems.

The goal of this roadmap is to define the potential product categories that can be developed using the AeroStitch Core structural system.

---

# Platform Vision

StitchCore is developing a new class of rigid inflatable human support systems.

These systems combine:

high-pressure drop-stitch structural technology  
lightweight portability  
rapid deployment capability  
modular accessory integration  

This platform architecture enables inflatable systems to replace heavier rigid equipment in multiple industries.

---

# Product Family Overview

The StitchCore platform can support multiple product categories across healthcare, sports, emergency response, and consumer wellness markets.

These product categories are outlined below.

---

# Sidekick Air
Portable Therapy Platform

Sidekick Air is the first commercial product built using AeroStitch Core Technology.

The platform is designed for:

manual therapists  
sports recovery teams  
mobile treatment professionals  
event recovery environments  

Key features include:

rigid inflatable treatment surface  
collapsible mechanical support stand  
modular accessory integration  
travel-ready portability  

Sidekick Air represents the entry point into the StitchCore ecosystem.

---

# Sidekick Rescue
Emergency Response Stretcher

Sidekick Rescue is a portable inflatable stretcher designed for emergency response and field rescue environments.

Potential applications include:

mountain rescue  
wilderness medicine  
disaster response  
military field operations  

Key characteristics may include:

lightweight deployable stretcher platform  
rapid inflation deployment  
compact transport configuration  
reinforced carry handles  
equipment attachment points  

Sidekick Rescue leverages the portability advantages of AeroStitch Core technology in difficult environments.

---

# Platform Advantages

All StitchCore products share several core advantages.

These include:

lightweight portability  
compact travel configuration  
rapid deployment capability  
high structural rigidity when inflated  
modular accessory integration  

These advantages make the platform uniquely suited for environments where traditional rigid equipment is difficult to transport.

# Strategic Market Opportunities

Several large markets intersect with the StitchCore platform.

mobile therapy professionals

emergency and rescue operations
