# StitchCore Platform Definition
Author: Drew Freedman  
Date: March 2026

## Purpose

This document establishes the official architecture and terminology for the StitchCore platform and its associated technologies and products.

Maintaining consistent terminology is important for documentation, intellectual property filings, manufacturing discussions, and future product development.

---

# Platform Architecture

The StitchCore ecosystem is organized into three layers.

StitchCore (Company and Platform)

AeroStitch Core Technology (Structural Technology)

Sidekick Air (Product)

This structure separates the company, the underlying engineering system, and the individual commercial products.

---

# StitchCore

StitchCore is the parent company and platform responsible for developing inflatable structural support systems designed for human use.

The StitchCore platform focuses on lightweight, portable, high-strength inflatable structures capable of supporting the human body in clinical, athletic, emergency, and consumer environments.

StitchCore represents the long-term technology and product ecosystem.

Future product categories may include:

portable therapy platforms  
sports recovery systems  
emergency stretchers  
medical gurneys  
field treatment platforms  
portable rehabilitation systems  
consumer recovery products

Sidekick Air represents the first product built within the StitchCore platform.

---

# AeroStitch Core Technology

AeroStitch Core Technology is the structural engineering system developed by StitchCore.

This technology uses high-pressure drop-stitch textile construction to create rigid inflatable platforms capable of supporting significant loads while remaining lightweight and portable.

Key characteristics include:

woven drop-stitch internal structural matrix  
TPU-coated textile layers  
high-pressure structural inflation  
rigid load-bearing performance  
modular attachment interface integration  

AeroStitch Core Technology enables inflatable structures to function as rigid support platforms suitable for clinical and structural applications.

This technology forms the engineering foundation for all StitchCore products.

---

# Sidekick Air

Sidekick Air is the first commercial product built using AeroStitch Core Technology.

The product is a portable inflatable therapy platform designed to support professional manual therapy, sports recovery treatments, and mobile treatment environments.

Sidekick Air demonstrates the capabilities of the AeroStitch Core structural platform.

The product integrates:

a rigid inflatable treatment surface  
a collapsible mechanical support stand  
a modular accessory interface system  
a portable travel configuration  

Sidekick Air represents the initial market entry product for the StitchCore platform.

---

# Platform Expansion

The StitchCore platform is designed to support a range of future products built using AeroStitch Core Technology.

Potential product extensions include:

portable rescue stretchers  
lightweight medical transport platforms  
field treatment surfaces  
sports recovery stations  
consumer wellness recovery platforms  

By building products on a shared structural technology platform, StitchCore enables scalable innovation across multiple industries.
