# Publishing Philosophy

The Tao of Clinical Touch generates publications across multiple formats, audiences, and purposes:

- **Books** — Comprehensive treatments of major themes
- **Journal Papers** — Peer-reviewed research and frameworks
- **Clinical Articles** — Practitioner-focused guidance
- **White Papers** — Research and position statements
- **Case Reports** — Real clinical stories illustrating principles
- **Educational Materials** — Curriculum and student texts

Every publication advances the central intellectual project: understanding and teaching permission-based clinical practice.
