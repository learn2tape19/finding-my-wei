# Speaking Philosophy

Speaking is not promotion. It is leadership.

When you speak, you:
- Shape what people think about your work
- Create permission for others to evolve
- Model the thinking you teach
- Build relationships and trust
- Generate energy and momentum

The speaking roadmap is how ideas move from "book" → "field conversation" → "standard practice."

---

# SPEAKING STRATEGY

## Core Messages by Audience

### For Clinical Practitioners
**"Permission is the missing ingredient in your toolkit."**
- You have technique. You have anatomy knowledge. You understand biomechanics.
- What's missing: Understanding the nervous system state that determines whether technique works.
- Permission is what you're already trying to create. Now you can be more intentional about it.

### For Educators
**"How you teach determines what kind of practitioners your students become."**
- If you teach through permission, your students become permission-based practitioners.
- This isn't about adding one more thing to curriculum.
- It's about teaching the same material through a different lens.

### For Researchers
**"Manual therapy is ready for alliance-based research."**
- Psychotherapy proved alliance > technique decades ago.
- Neuroscience now explains why.
- Manual therapy research is ready to test this in our modality.
- You can be the ones who advance the field.

### For Healthcare Systems
**"Permission-based practice improves outcomes and patient retention."**
- Practitioners trained in permission build stronger alliances.
- Better alliances predict better outcomes.
- Better outcomes drive retention.
- This is economically rational.

### For Students
**"Your nervous system is your primary tool."**
- Technique is secondary.
- Before you master anything else, master your own regulation.
- That's what creates permission for others.

# SPEAKING INFRASTRUCTURE

## Materials & Support
- Professional speaker one-sheet
- Video clip library (from events)
- Client testimonial videos
- Research findings summaries
- Publication list and links
- Media kit and photos
- Easy speaker agreement/contract process

## Logistics
- Speaker coordinator (or system for managing inquiries)
- Standardized bio and abstracts (various lengths)
- Tech check procedures
- Presentation formats (keynote, 90min workshop, 60min, 30min)
- Virtual speaking capability

## Financial Model
- No speaking fees for year 1-2 (build platform)
- Year 2+: Develop fee structure (often waived for professional conferences; charged for corporate)
- Speaking can drive CE enrollment (primary revenue)
- Travel budget: $5-10k/year
