# Relationship Management Principles

1. **Expertise-Focused:** Each advisor contributes in their area of expertise
2. **Strategic:** Relationships are cultivated for long-term value, not transactional projects
3. **Coordinated:** All advisors align through the Founder
4. **Transparent:** Clear communication about expectations, timelines, and decisions
5. **Respectful:** Advisors are treated as strategic partners, not vendors
6. **Documented:** Decisions and relationships are preserved in institutional record
