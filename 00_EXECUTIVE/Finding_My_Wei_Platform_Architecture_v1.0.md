## THE FOUR PRIMARY ENGINES

### ENGINE 1: ATLAS (Research & Intellectual Capital)

**What it does:** Translates clinical research into actionable frameworks

**Inputs:**
- Literature review (40+ papers per project)
- Clinical observation and practice patterns
- Nervous system science integration
- Outcome research synthesis

**Outputs:**
- Project briefs (strategic research summary)
- Clinical reasoning frameworks (how-to-think guides)
- Evidence matrices (research organized by claim)
- Teaching materials (lecture content, discussion guides)

**Constraints:**
- All claims traceable to cited research
- Clinical terminology precise and defensible
- No marketing language
- Frameworks grounded in nervous system mechanics

**Status:** 🟢 Operational — Project Atlas 01 (Therapeutic Alliance) in progress

---

### ENGINE 2: MEDIA OPERATING SYSTEM (Publishing & Distribution)

**What it does:** Transforms one research idea into 15+ coordinated assets, publishes them strategically, measures results, learns from data

**Components:**
1. **Governance** (Editorial Constitution, Standards Manual, Decision Trees)
2. **Content Strategy** (Campaign planning, asset mapping, audience identification)
3. **Publishing** (Platform strategy, cross-linking, repurposing, scheduling)
4. **Analytics** (Metrics, measurement, reflection, learning integration)

**Inputs:** 
- Intellectual capital from Atlas
- Campaign plan (type, scope, audience, timeline)
- Asset specifications (dimensions, tone, platform adaptation)

**Outputs:**
- Website articles (long-form, SEO-optimized)
- Social content (platform-adapted, platform-timed)
- Email sequences (nurture, education, CTA)
- Video scripts & storyboards
- Teaching materials & discussion guides
- Publishing schedule (coordinated timeline)
- Analytics plan (what to measure, when, why)

**Constraints:**
- No promotional content above 3% total
- All content education-first
- All assets must pass Editorial Review Checklist
- Analytics data must flow back to Reflection Engine

**Status:** 🟢 Operational & Validated — Campaign 001 ready for execution

---

### ENGINE 3: VISUAL OPERATING SYSTEM (Design & Rendering)

**What it does:** Ensures all visual content maintains constitutional values through a 9-layer inheritance hierarchy

**Layers (Frozen):**
1. **Layer 00: Constitution** — "Design serves ideas, never competes"
2. **Layer 01: Principles** — Editorial restraint, clarity, accessibility, timelessness
3. **Layer 02: Tokens** — Design variables (typography, spacing, color, radius) — LOCKED
4. **Layer 03: Rules** — Behavioral constraints (how tokens must be used)
5. **Layer 04: Components** — Reusable building blocks per project
6. **Layer 05: Compositions** — Page layouts per project
7. **Layer 06: Implementations** — Software renderings (Adobe Express, Figma, PowerPoint, Web)
8. **Layer 07: Outputs** — Final exports (Instagram, PDF, website, print)
9. **Layer 08: Intelligence** — Analytics & learning feedback loop

**Key Feature:** Inheritance is inviolable. No layer can override parent layer.

**Status:** 🟢 Frozen — 9-layer architecture locked, Layer 02 tokens immutable globally

---

### ENGINE 4: REFLECTION ENGINE (Learning & Continuous Improvement)

**What it does:** Captures performance data, identifies patterns, proposes system improvements

**Process:**
1. **Collect** — Analytics from outputs
2. **Analyze** — Pattern identification
3. **Reflect** — Post-campaign reflection
4. **Propose** — Evidence-based refinements
5. **Feed back** — Learning integration

**Data Sources:**
- MOS analytics (engagement, platform metrics, audience response)
- VOS performance (design clarity, accessibility feedback)
- Atlas impact (clinician adoption, educator interest)
- Knowledge Graph queries

**Constraint:** Intelligence cannot override Constitution (values protect against reactivity)

**Status:** 🟢 Ready — Template created, first reflection pending post-Campaign 001
