# Publishing Domain Charter

## Authority
Established by Drew Freedman to preserve and evolve the Boston Bodyworker platform.

## Purpose
This domain manages **The Boston Bodyworker**, a 30+ year publishing platform and authority asset.

The Boston Bodyworker is not a legacy archive. It is an appreciating intellectual asset representing decades of professional authority, domain expertise, and educational publishing.

## Scope

### Inclusion Criteria
- Website and digital presence
- Published writing and articles
- Essays and educational content
- Newsletter and communications
- Media and professional photos
- Credentials, testimonials, and authority documentation
- Historical content with continuing value
- Publishing operations and metadata

### Exclusion Criteria
- Founder's personal work → goes to FOUNDER_DOMAIN
- Cross-domain research → goes to 02_PROJECT_ATLAS
- Marketing campaigns (unless published as content) → goes to 04_CAPABILITIES/MARKETING
- Operations shared with other domains → goes to 04_CAPABILITIES/OPERATIONS
- Permanent frameworks → goes to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE/FRAMEWORKS

## Governance

**Authority:** Drew Freedman (steward), with delegation to publishing director when appointed

**Decisions:**
- Content strategy and direction: Steward decides
- Publishing schedule and focus: Steward decides
- Historical content preservation: Steward decides
- Website evolution: Steward decides
- Movement of work to permanent estate: Steward approves

**Decision Speed:** Deliberate (authority asset requires care)

## Lifecycle States

- **CREATION** → Content is being written
- **REFINEMENT** → Content is being edited and reviewed
- **STABLE** → Content is published and maintained
- **PUBLICATION** → Content is widely shared
- **PRESERVATION** → Significant work moves to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE
