# Innovation Domain Charter

## Authority
Established by Drew Freedman to manage StitchCore, the innovation and product organization.

## Purpose
This domain manages **StitchCore**, the innovation and product development organization.

This domain creates new products, technologies, and intellectual property. It manages engineering, patents, manufacturing, and product launches. It is designed to scale with multiple products while maintaining core technology and manufacturing relationships.

## Scope

### Inclusion Criteria
- Products and product development
- Technology and core innovations (AeroStitch)
- Patents and intellectual property
- Engineering specifications and standards
- Manufacturing relationships and processes
- Supplier and OEM partnerships
- Product strategy and roadmaps
- Operations and project management

### Exclusion Criteria
- Marketing and product positioning → goes to 04_CAPABILITIES/MARKETING
- Finance and investor relations → goes to 04_CAPABILITIES/OPERATIONS (or local operations)
- Research (pre-product) → goes to 02_PROJECT_ATLAS
- Published technology papers → goes to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE/PAPERS
- Permanent engineering standards → goes to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE/METHODOLOGIES

## Governance

**Authority:** Drew Freedman (steward), with delegation to innovation director when appointed

**Decisions:**
- Product strategy: Steward and director decide
- Engineering standards: Chief engineer and director decide
- Patent strategy: Steward with legal counsel
- Manufacturing relationships: Director with steward approval
- Product launch: Director proposes, steward approves
- Future product scope: Steward decides

**Decision Speed:** As-needed (product decisions require speed)

## Lifecycle States

- **CREATION** → Product is being conceived and designed
- **REFINEMENT** → Product is being engineered and tested
- **STABLE** → Product is ready for manufacturing
- **PUBLICATION** → Product is launched and in market
- **PRESERVATION** → Product technology becomes permanent IP asset
