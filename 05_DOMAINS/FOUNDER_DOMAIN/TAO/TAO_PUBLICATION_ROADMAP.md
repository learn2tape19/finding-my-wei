# Publishing Philosophy

The Tao generates publications across multiple formats to serve different audiences and purposes:

- **Books** — Comprehensive treatments of major themes
- **Journal Papers** — Peer-reviewed evidence and frameworks
- **White Papers** — Research and position statements
- **Case Reports** — Clinical stories illustrating principles
- **Educational Materials** — Curriculum and student texts
- **Thought Leadership** — Articles, editorials, media

Every publication should:
- Advance understanding of the Governing Question
- Illuminate one or more Intellectual Pillars
- Contribute to answering Research Questions from TAO_DOMAIN.md
- Remain grounded in Permission (Pillar 2) as organizing principle
- Honor evidence and remain open to contradiction
