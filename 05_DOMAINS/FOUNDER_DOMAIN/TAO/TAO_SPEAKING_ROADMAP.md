# Relationship to Domain

This roadmap shows how the intellectual domain becomes public communication.

It does not replace TAO_DOMAIN.md.

It answers: "How do we reach practitioners, educators, researchers, and healthcare leaders with these ideas?"

Speaking is how the domain scales beyond those who can read.

---

# Speaking Philosophy

Speaking is not promotion.

It is leadership.

When speaking, we:
- Shape what people think about clinical practice
- Create permission for others to evolve
- Model the thinking we teach
- Build relationships and trust
- Generate energy for field transformation

---

# Core Messages by Audience

**To Practitioners:** "Permission is what you're already trying to create. Now you can be intentional about it."

**To Educators:** "How you teach determines what kind of practitioners your students become."

**To Researchers:** "Manual therapy research is ready for alliance-based investigations."

**To Healthcare Systems:** "Permission-based practice improves outcomes and patient retention."

**To Students:** "Your nervous system is your primary tool."

# Platform Building Progression

**Years 1-2: Foundation**
- Regional state conferences
- Educator network presentations
- University guest lectures
- Build speaking infrastructure

**Years 2-4: Growth**
- Major conference keynotes
- Regular podcast presence
- Media inquiry pipeline
- Specialized speaking tracks

**Years 4+: Maturity**
- Recognized thought leader
- Regular keynote invitations
- Media influence and presence
- Mentoring emerging voices
- Field leadership position

# What This Speaking Roadmap Represents

Speaking is how ideas escape books.

A book reaches 5,000 readers in a year.

A keynote reaches 2,000 in 90 minutes.

A podcast reaches 1,000s over months.

A conversation changes one person.

Together, speaking transforms perspectives, curricula, conferences, and field direction.
