# Founder Domain Charter

## Authority
Established by Drew Freedman. Drew Freedman is the steward of this domain.

## Purpose
This domain preserves **Drew Freedman's creative work, thinking, and intellectual development**.

This is where ideas originate. This is where concepts are explored. This is where the workshop operates.

## Scope

### Inclusion Criteria
- Personal philosophy and principles
- Writings, journals, and reflections
- Speaking engagements, interviews, and media appearances
- In-development projects and creative work
- Decision history and strategic thinking
- Relationships and network documentation
- Origin stories and biography
- Personal vision and direction

### Exclusion Criteria
- Completed, published work → goes to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE
- Cross-domain research → goes to 02_PROJECT_ATLAS
- Operational decisions affecting multiple domains → goes to Constitutional layer
- Marketing and positioning → goes to 04_CAPABILITIES/MARKETING (or domain-specific)
- Infrastructure and tools → goes to 01_OPERATING_SYSTEM or 04_CAPABILITIES

## Governance

**Authority:** Drew Freedman (steward)

**Decisions:**
- What goes in this domain: Drew Freedman decides
- When work moves to INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE: Drew Freedman decides
- Public vs. private content: Drew Freedman decides
- Amendment of this charter: Drew Freedman approves

**Decision Speed:** Immediate (Drew's domain, Drew's speed)

## Lifecycle States

- **CREATION** → Idea is born, draft is written
- **REFINEMENT** → Idea is developed, feedback is gathered
- **STABLE** → Work is finished, ready to share
- **PUBLICATION** → Work is published or widely shared
- **PRESERVATION** → Significant work moves to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE
