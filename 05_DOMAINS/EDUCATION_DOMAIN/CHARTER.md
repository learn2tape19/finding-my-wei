# Education Domain Charter

## Authority
Established by Drew Freedman to manage Learn2Tape, the education organization.

## Purpose
This domain manages **Learn2Tape**, the online education platform.

This domain creates and delivers educational products, certifications, and continuing education. It focuses on teaching massage therapists and related professionals.

## Scope

### Inclusion Criteria
- Courses and course materials
- Curriculum and learning design
- Certifications and credentials
- Teaching materials and methodologies
- Student success frameworks and metrics
- LMS configuration and management
- Products (K-Cuts, courses, certificates)
- Educational research and innovation
- Operations and student services

### Exclusion Criteria
- Research that serves multiple domains → goes to 02_PROJECT_ATLAS
- Marketing and campaigns → goes to 04_CAPABILITIES/MARKETING (or local subcopy)
- Permanent frameworks → goes to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE/FRAMEWORKS
- Payment processing and finance → goes to 04_CAPABILITIES/OPERATIONS
- Teaching methodology (when generalized) → goes to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE/METHODOLOGIES

## Governance

**Authority:** Drew Freedman (steward), with delegation to education director when appointed

**Decisions:**
- Course curriculum: Director and instructors decide
- Certification requirements: Director and steward decide
- Teaching methodology: Instructors and director decide
- Student success initiatives: Director decides
- Technology and platform: Director with steward approval
- Movement of work to permanent estate: Director proposes, steward approves

**Decision Speed:** As-needed (operational domain)

## Lifecycle States

- **CREATION** → Course is in development
- **REFINEMENT** → Course is being tested and improved
- **STABLE** → Course is active and maintained
- **PUBLICATION** → Course is widely enrolled and recognized
- **PRESERVATION** → Teaching methodology becomes permanent framework
