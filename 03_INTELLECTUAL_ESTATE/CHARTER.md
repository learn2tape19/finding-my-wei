# Intellectual Estate Layer Charter

## Authority
Established by Drew Freedman to preserve enduring intellectual work.

## Purpose
This layer is the **permanent library** of completed, validated, published knowledge.

The Intellectual Estate is read-mostly. It preserves:
- Completed books and publications
- Peer-reviewed research
- Proven frameworks and methodologies
- Decision models and principles
- Historical and strategic records

## Scope

### Inclusion Criteria
- Published books and manuscripts
- Peer-reviewed research papers
- Validated frameworks and methodologies
- Permanent decision records
- Historical documentation with continuing value
- Award-winning or recognized works
- Foundational principles and models

### Exclusion Criteria
- Working drafts and notes → go to DOMAINS or PROJECT_ATLAS
- Operational procedures → go to 01_OPERATING_SYSTEM
- Incomplete research → stays in 02_PROJECT_ATLAS
- Marketing materials (unless published as content) → go to 04_CAPABILITIES/MARKETING
- Temporary decisions → go to domain DECISION_LOGS

## Governance

**Authority:** Drew Freedman (steward), Claude (librarian)

**Placement Process:**
1. Asset is identified as permanent knowledge
2. Quality is validated (through review process)
3. Appropriate category is determined (BOOKS, PAPERS, FRAMEWORKS, etc.)
4. Asset is catalogued and preserved
5. Lifecycle state is marked as PRESERVATION

**Amendment Speed:** Rare (changes only through formal review)

## Major Categories

### BOOKS/
Published books and major published works.
- The-Tao-of-Clinical-Touch (published)
- Future books as they complete

### PAPERS/
Peer-reviewed research papers and formal studies.
Typically derived from PROJECT_ATLAS work.

### FRAMEWORKS/
Validated methodologies, models, and frameworks.
Teaching methods, decision frameworks, clinical frameworks.

### METHODOLOGIES/
Formal processes and procedures that have proven value.
How we conduct research, teach, develop products.

### DECISION_LOGS/
Permanent record of major decisions and their rationale.
Strategic choices, pivots, architectural decisions.

### ARCHIVE/
Historical work with continuing value for context or history.
Not actively used but preserved for completeness.

## Lifecycle States
- **PRESERVATION** → Permanent state (default for this layer)
- **ARCHIVE** → Historical reference (still preserved, not active)

(Nothing in INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE is CREATION or REFINEMENT)

## Relationships
- **Receives from:** 02_PROJECT_ATLAS (synthesized research)
- **Receives from:** 05_DOMAINS (completed work)
- **Serves:** All domains (reference and guidance)
- **Uses services:** 04_CAPABILITIES/SYNTHESIS, QUALITY_ASSURANCE
- **Reported to:** 00_CONSTITUTION (permanent record)

## Success Metrics
- Estate contains only validated, completed work
- Every asset is discoverable and documented
- Historical context is preserved
- Frameworks guide current work
- Estate grows deliberately, not chaotically
- Future collaborators can understand decades of work
