# Engineering Research

Technical findings, materials research, manufacturing intelligence, and engineering principles.

## Research Categories

### Lightweight Materials
- Aluminum alloys (properties, availability, cost)
- Composites (carbon fiber, fiber-reinforced polymers)
- Titanium (when justified, cost/benefit)
- Structural properties, weight, durability

### Folding Mechanisms
- Scissor mechanisms (load distribution, complexity)
- Hinged designs (strength, symmetry)
- Telescoping systems (smoothness, repeatability)
- Locking mechanisms (quick-release, reliability)

### Deployable Systems
- Military cot designs (proven reliability, weight optimization)
- Aerospace deployable structures (precision, safety)
- Emergency shelter systems (rapid deployment, durability)
- Industrial platform design

### Manufacturing Techniques
- Tube bending capabilities (sizes, materials, tolerances)
- Aluminum extrusion (economies of scale, customization)
- TIG welding (aluminum-specific challenges)
- Assembly automation (for volume production)
- Quality control standards (medical device requirements)

### Medical Device Standards
- FDA requirements (if applicable)
- ISO standards (medical devices, manufacturing)
- Biocompatibility requirements
- Load testing standards
