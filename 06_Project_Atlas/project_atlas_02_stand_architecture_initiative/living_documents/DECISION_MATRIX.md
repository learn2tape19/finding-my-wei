# Decision Matrix

Record of major decisions made during SAI, with reasoning and review dates.

Uses the Finding My Wei [[DECISION_ENGINE.md]] format.

---

## Decision Template

```markdown
## [Decision Title]

**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD  
**Owner**: [Who made the decision]  
**Status**: [Active/Archived/Revisited]  
**Review Date**: YYYY-MM-DD

### Context
[What situation forced this decision?]

### Alternatives Considered
1. [Option A: description, pros, cons, why not chosen]
2. [Option B: description, pros, cons, why not chosen]
3. [Option C: description, pros, cons, why not chosen]

### Decision
[Chosen option and final reasoning]

### Tradeoffs
**We gained**: [What becomes possible]  
**We sacrificed**: [What we gave up]

### Expected Outcome
[What should happen if this decision is right]

### Review Schedule
[When to revisit this decision]
```
