## Outreach Strategy

When contacting prospects, remember:

**Never ask**: "Can you build my stand?"

**Instead say**: "We are seeking an engineering partner to help develop the structural architecture of an innovative lightweight deployable platform for medical and therapeutic applications."

This attracts engineering partners, not manufacturing vendors.

---

## Contact Status Definitions

- **Not Contacted**: Prospect identified, not yet reached out
- **Contacted**: Initial contact made, waiting for response
- **Responded**: Prospect responded, conversation continuing
- **Meeting Scheduled**: Call or in-person meeting arranged
- **Technical Discussion**: Deep technical conversation ongoing
- **Qualified**: Partner fit confirmed, moving toward agreement
- **Disqualified**: Not a fit, moving on to next prospect
- **Inactive**: Was promising but became unresponsive
