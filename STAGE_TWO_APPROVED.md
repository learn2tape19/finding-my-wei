## 4. Research: Source in Google Drive, Synthesis in Project Atlas
**Architecture:**
- Working materials: Stay in Google Drive (where active research happens)
- Synthesis: Moved to Project Atlas (synthesized knowledge)
- Publications: Moved to Intellectual Estate (when published)

**Rationale:**
- Keeps active workflow efficient (don't move working materials)
- Brings synthesized knowledge into estate (what's permanent)
- Preserves natural research workflow

---

## 5. RETIRED Lifecycle State (New)
**Used for:** Legacy folders after successful ingestion

**Meaning:** "This folder was successfully ingested. All knowledge mapped. Folder is archived but kept as reference."

**Distinction:**
- ARCHIVE state: Old work *within* a domain (e.g., old courses)
- RETIRED state: Legacy folder structure *after ingestion* (e.g., Learn2Tape/ folder is RETIRED after content moves to EDUCATION_DOMAIN)

---

## 6. INGESTION_LOG.md (Complete Record)
**Records:** Every migration, every decision, every relationship preserved

**Purpose:**
- Traceability (what moved where and when)
- Decision documentation (why each choice was made)
- Verification checklist (confirming completion)
- Learnings archive (what worked, what didn't)

**Outcome:** Evolution of the estate becomes part of its permanent history
