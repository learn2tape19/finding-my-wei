## SYSTEM WEAKNESSES IDENTIFIED

1. **Documentation Density** — MOS creates many documents; requires discipline to reference them consistently

2. **Initial Overhead** — First campaign requires reading/understanding MOS; subsequent campaigns easier

3. **Role Clarity for Small Teams** — Perfect for team of 5, but how to adapt if fewer people?

4. **Platform Evolution** — MOS won't anticipate new platforms (TikTok today, unknown tomorrow)

5. **Measurement Precision** — Analytics framework is solid but implementation depends on tool capabilities
