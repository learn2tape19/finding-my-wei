# Constitution Layer Charter

## Authority
Established by Drew Freedman on the authority of the Repository Constitution v2.0.

## Purpose
This layer is the **permanent governing charter** for Finding My Wei.

It defines:
- The nature of the system (Intellectual Estate Management System)
- The organizational principles (entity-driven, not project-driven)
- The roles and authorities (Steward, Librarian, Repository)
- The amendment process
- Non-negotiable principles

## Scope

### Inclusion Criteria
- Constitutional charter (CONSTITUTION.md)
- Governance procedures (GOVERNANCE.md)
- Amendment history (AMENDMENTS.md)
- Authority definitions (AUTHORITY.md)
- Foundational directives

### Exclusion Criteria
- Operational procedures → go to 01_OPERATING_SYSTEM
- Specific domain governance → goes to 05_DOMAINS/[domain]/CHARTER.md
- Capability standards → goes to 04_CAPABILITIES/[service]/CHARTER.md
- Research documents → go to 02_PROJECT_ATLAS
- Completed knowledge → goes to 03_INTELLECTUAL_ESTATE

## Governance

**Authority:** Drew Freedman (Steward)

**Amendment Process:**
1. Change is proposed with rationale
2. Constitutional impact is assessed
3. Drew Freedman reviews and decides
4. Amendment is documented in AMENDMENTS.md
5. Effective date is recorded

**Decision Speed:** Deliberate (changes are rare)

## Lifecycle States
Not applicable. Constitutional documents are permanent until formally amended.

## Relationships
- **Governs:** All other layers (01, 02, 03, 04, 05)
- **Serves:** Entire IEMS system
- **Reports to:** Drew Freedman (ultimate authority)

## Success Metrics
- Constitution is understood by all collaborators
- Changes are rare and thoughtful
- Amendments reflect learning, not convenience
- System remains coherent across years
