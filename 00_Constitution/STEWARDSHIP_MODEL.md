# Purpose

The purpose of Finding My Wei is not to preserve information.

Its purpose is to **cultivate wisdom that can be shared, tested, refined, and ultimately carried forward**.

This document defines how the Intellectual Estate is stewarded.

**Architecture** defines where knowledge lives.

**Stewardship** defines how knowledge lives.

---

# Stewardship Philosophy

Finding My Wei is a **living intellectual ecosystem**.

Knowledge is never considered finished.

It continually evolves through observation, research, publication, feedback, and reflection.

Every contribution should strengthen the estate.

Nothing of lasting value should depend upon a single technology, platform, or AI.

**Roles endure.**

**Tools change.**

**Knowledge compounds.**

---

# Stewardship Principle

We do not preserve files.

**We preserve relationships.**

Every asset derives its meaning from its relationships.

Those relationships may include:

- People
- Domains
- Capabilities
- Projects
- Investigations
- Publications
- Decisions
- Conversations
- Principles
- Historical context

Whenever possible, preserve the relationships before organizing the files.

# Conversation Stewardship

Conversations are **not** considered repository assets.

They are **source material**.

Every significant conversation should be evaluated for transformation into:

- Principles
- Frameworks
- Decision Logs
- Publications
- Research Questions
- Project Updates
- Historical Records
- Operating Procedures

**Only refined knowledge enters the Intellectual Estate.**

---

# Relationship Stewardship

Whenever new knowledge is added, identify:

- What influenced it?
- What investigations support it?
- Which Domain owns it?
- Which Capability enables it?
- Which publications reference it?
- Which decisions depend upon it?

**Relationships create meaning.**

---

# Quality Stewardship

Before knowledge is considered stable, evaluate:

- Accuracy
- Clarity
- Evidence
- Historical context
- Strategic value
- Reusability
- Discoverability
- Publication potential

**Knowledge should mature before permanent preservation.**

# Succession Principle

Finding My Wei is designed to **outlive individual tools**.

If one AI system is replaced, stewardship responsibilities transfer.

**Roles persist.**

**Platforms evolve.**

**The Intellectual Estate remains continuous.**

The architecture must never depend upon one vendor, one technology, or one assistant.

---

# Constitutional Integrity

No steward may violate the Repository Constitution.

If uncertainty exists:

**The Constitution prevails.**

**Seek clarification before implementing change.**
