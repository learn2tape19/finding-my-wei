## Decision 001

**Date:** July 9, 2026

**Decision:** Permanent prohibition on em dashes

**Authority:** Founder + Editorial Board

**Applies To:** Entire institution

**Reasoning:**
- Em dashes create ambiguity about punctuation intent
- Forcing choice between comma, semicolon, or period produces clearer writing
- Improves pacing by eliminating parenthetical asides
- Better reflects Tao voice (direct, not digressive)
- Cleaner typography without visual complexity

**Implementation:**
- All new publications checked for em dashes
- All em dashes replaced with semicolons or periods
- PUNCTUATION_STANDARD.md mandates this rule
- Pre-publication checklist verifies compliance

**Status:** Permanent. No reversal without evidence of material limitation.

## Decision 002

**Date:** July 9, 2026

**Decision:** Story before explanation is non-negotiable structure

**Authority:** Founder + Editorial Board

**Applies To:** All publications

**Reasoning:**
- Readers remember stories more than abstract concepts
- Stories create curiosity, which primes the reader to learn
- Scientific explanation landing after story creates deeper understanding
- Invites reader into the clinician's experience before asking them to learn theory
- Matches how real clinical thinking develops (observation → pattern → theory)

**Implementation:**
- Every article leads with clinical observation or story
- Theory and explanation follow, not precede
- EDITORIAL_STANDARDS.md mandates this structure
- Editorial Board review verifies compliance

**Status:** Permanent. Core principle of Finding My Wei voice.

## Decision 003

**Date:** July 9, 2026

**Decision:** Curiosity before certainty is mandatory stance

**Authority:** Founder + Editorial Board

**Applies To:** All publications

**Reasoning:**
- Absolute statements damage scientific credibility over time
- Hedged language ("may," "might," "appears to") invites reader reflection
- Uncertainty respects complexity of human biology and clinical practice
- Curious tone encourages readers to think, not just absorb
- Protects institutional credibility when future science evolves

**Implementation:**
- SCIENTIFIC_INTEGRITY_STANDARD.md defines five-level certainty framework
- Claims must be proportional to evidence
- No unsupported mechanisms or biological claims
- Editorial Board review verifies proportionality

**Status:** Permanent. Non-negotiable scientific principle.

## Decision 005

**Date:** July 9, 2026

**Decision:** BLOG directory is canonical publication source

**Authority:** Founder + Editorial Board

**Applies To:** All publications

**Reasoning:**
- BLOG/PRODUCTION confusion of July 9 demonstrated cost of ambiguity
