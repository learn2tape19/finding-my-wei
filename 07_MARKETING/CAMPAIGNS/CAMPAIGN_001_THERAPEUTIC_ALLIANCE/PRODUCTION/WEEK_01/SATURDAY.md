### Facebook Post

Wu Wei is often translated as effortless action.

In the treatment room, it means something even more practical.

Don't impose. Observe.
Listen. Match. Respond.

The body already knows how to reorganize.

Our work isn't to force that reorganization. Our work is to stop giving the nervous system reasons not to.

Watch what happens when you stop trying to make something happen and start responding to what's actually there.

Force creates resistance. Observation creates clarity. Waiting creates information. Matching creates permission.

And permission creates change.

Wu Wei isn't about doing nothing. It's about not doing the wrong thing. About being intelligent enough to let the wisdom that's already present emerge.

———

What's one moment where doing less created a better outcome?
