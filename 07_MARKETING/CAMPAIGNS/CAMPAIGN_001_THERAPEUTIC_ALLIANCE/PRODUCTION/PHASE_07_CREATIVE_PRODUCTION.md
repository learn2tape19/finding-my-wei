## Adobe Express Layout Specifications

### Standard Instagram Post (1080×1350px)

**Layout Grid:**
1. **Header Area (0-200px):**
   - Logo or day identifier (Tao mark, small)
   - Date and day of week (right-aligned, small Manrope Regular)

2. **Image Area (200-1100px):**
   - Full-width Firefly hero image (centered)
   - No crop; preserve entire composition

3. **Text Overlay (900-1350px):**
   - Primary headline: Manrope Bold, 48px, white, center/left-aligned
   - Secondary text: Manrope Regular, 28px, white with 60% opacity
   - Dark gradient overlay (bottom 40% of image) for text legibility
   - 30px padding from edges

4. **Footer Area (bottom 50px):**
   - Call-to-action: "Link in bio" or specific CTA (Manrope Regular, 16px)
   - Tao Visual Accent (small colored element if needed)

**Color Palette:**
- Background: Black (#000000) or transparent
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) primary, white with 60% opacity for secondary
- Accent: Clinical teal or warm accent color (from Tao brand system)
- Dark Gradient: Linear gradient from transparent (top) to black (bottom, 60% opacity)

---

### Instagram Carousel Slides (5 slides per day, 1080×1350px each)

**Slide Structure (consistent across all 5 slides):**

1. **Slide 1:** Headline concept
   - Large centered text (60px Manrope Bold)
   - Hero image faded in background (30% opacity)
   - CTA: "Swipe" or slide indicator

2. **Slides 2-4:** Key points (one concept per slide)
   - Text-focused design
   - Supporting image (40% opacity) or solid color background
   - Point statement in large text (48px Manrope Bold)
   - Supporting text (24px Manrope Regular)

3. **Slide 5:** Call-to-action & link
   - Day's theme summarized
   - Blog link button or QR code
   - Save/Share messaging

**Example Carousel for Thursday:**
- Slide 1: "Permission Changes Everything"
- Slide 2: "Pressure isn't permission"
- Slide 3: "Permission is physiological"
- Slide 4: "Permission changes perception"
- Slide 5: "Read the full article → [QR code]"
