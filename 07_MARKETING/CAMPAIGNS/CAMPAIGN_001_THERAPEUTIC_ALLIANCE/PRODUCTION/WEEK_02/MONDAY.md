## Editorial Notes

This post establishes Week 2's central reframe: the greet is not administrative time. It is clinical intervention time.

**Do NOT use:**
- "Game changer"
- "Transform"
- "The secret"
- Em dashes
- Shame-based framing ("Most therapists get this wrong")

**Do use:**
- Observation-based opening
- Clinical language
- Direct, short sentences
- Reflection questions
