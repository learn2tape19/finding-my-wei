## SHARED VISUAL STANDARD (All Days)

All imagery must reflect authentic clinical realism and premium editorial healthcare documentary photography.

### Required Elements
- Warm natural window light (primary source)
- Authentic clinical environment (organized, minimal)
- Professional boundaries clearly visible
- Documentary observation (not performance)
- No stock photography aesthetic
- Clinician presence and attentiveness
- Patient comfort and regulation (where patient visible)

### Forbidden Elements
- Forced smiles, performance energy
- Spa aesthetics, candles, flowers, luxe styling
- Mystical or meditative clichés
- High-contrast dramatic lighting
- Heroic therapist imagery
- Stock photography quality
- Hands in "energy" gestures
- Multiple people (unless composition requires)
- Chinese characters or cultural appropriation

### Brand Layers
- Canonical Tao chapter symbols (pre-positioned, not recreated)
- Tao ripple watermark (5–8% opacity, partially cropped)
- Canonical Tao logo (small, subtle placement)
- Manrope typography (if text overlay required)
- Palette: Linen, warm white, oak, muted navy, sage, natural skin tones

## TEXT OVERLAY SPECIFICATIONS

**When Text Overlay Required:**

**Font:** Manrope (regular, semi-bold, or bold as needed)  
**Size:** Responsive to frame (minimum readable at mobile: 18pt equivalent)  
**Color:** White (primary), with subtle dark shadow for contrast  
**Placement:** Top third, lower third, or centered (never blocks focal point)  
**Background:** Transparent or subtle dark gradient (5–10% opacity) if text needs support  
**Lines:** Maximum 3–4 lines for readability  
**Alignment:** Center or left-align (not justified)  

**Stories (1080×1920px):**
- Text typically upper portion
- Safe area: 100px margins all sides

## PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

### Phase 1: Photography (Real Clinical Settings)
1. Identify authentic clinical moments matching each brief
2. Shoot with clinician(s) in actual workspace
3. Prioritize natural window light
4. Capture multiple frames per scene (minimum 5 angles per brief)

### Phase 2: Selection & Cropping
1. Select best frame per brief (authentic, professional, clinically real)
2. Crop to exact dimensions (1080×1350, 1200×628, 1080×1920)
3. Maintain safe area for text overlays
4. Verify no cropping eliminates focal point

### Phase 3: Branding Application
1. Add canonical Tao chapter symbol (SVG from brand assets, no recreation)
2. Add Tao ripple watermark (5–8% opacity, partially cropped per brand standard)
3. Add canonical Tao logo (small, subtle, placement: lower right or top left per context)
4. Maintain professional healthcare editorial appearance

### Phase 4: Text Overlay (If Required)
1. Position text per brief specifications
2. Use Manrope Bold or semi-bold
3. Apply subtle shadow for readability
4. Verify text doesn't obscure focal point

### Phase 5: Final QA
1. Verify dimensions (exact pixel measurements)
2. Verify color palette matches Tao Visual Identity
3. Verify no stock photography aesthetic
4. Verify clinical realism and authenticity
5. Verify brand assets correctly placed
6. Export for platform (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Blog)

### Phase 6: Alt Text Finalization
1. After image selection, write specific alt text describing actual image content
2. Replace [Pending...] placeholders with final alt text
3. Update COMPLETE_ASSET_INVENTORY.md with final alt text
